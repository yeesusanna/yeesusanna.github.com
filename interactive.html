Decade of Design

Interactive website will be about different occupations in the field of design, focusing on programmers, traditional graphic designers, web designers, and the salaries that they make. Information will be on a line graph that will be able to access information about the last decade along with events paired with some of the dates provided. Users will be able to view different occupations, a description, and be able to see their positions, rise, and fall on the line graph created. Users will also find out information about the current events that might not have caused the rise/fall but what may have happened at the time. Users may be able to compare and contrast two occupations of their choosing to de-clutter all lines on graph.

The usability for the graph, users will be able to pinpoint the occupation they are most interested in, and perform comparisons between the two. The user can also view information based on the graph and see the events that correspond to the date by hovering over the lines, and then a window will appear to provide more information.
